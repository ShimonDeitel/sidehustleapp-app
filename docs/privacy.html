Privacy Policy

Sidehustle is an offline-first app. All data you enter (Description, Amount, Type, and dates) is stored locally on your device in the app's private Application Support directory. Sidehustle does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, and does not use analytics or third-party tracking.

Purchases (subscription or one-time unlock) are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit; Sidehustle never sees your payment details.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it. There is no account, no server, and no data sync.
